The Basic Brain

  • The Brain is part of the body's central nervous system and because it's so complex, neuroscientists don't fully understand how it works.
  • The Brain is made up of around 90 billion neurons which send and receive infomation.
  • It is a myth that we use only 10% of our Brain. A healthy person will use all their Brain, even when they sleep they'll use more the 10% of their Brain.
